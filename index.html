Everything you need to know for your adventure

The best time for a safari in Kenya is during the dry seasons:

  • January to March - Excellent for game viewing, calving season
  • July to October - Great Migration, ideal weather

Our coastal adventures are perfect year-round, with December to March offering the warmest, clearest waters.

Absolutely! Our jet ski tours are designed for all skill levels:

  • Professional instructors accompany every tour
  • Multiple route options from gentle to thrilling

Booking your adventure is simple:

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp, email, or our form above
  2. Consultation - Our concierge team discusses your preferences
  3. Custom proposal - Receive a tailored itinerary within 24 hours
  4. Confirm & pay - Secure booking with 25% deposit

For private charters and special occasions, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance during peak season.

Your safety is our top priority:

  • All guides are certified and wilderness-trained
